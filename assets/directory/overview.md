# Shopify Source Sync Reference App

Synchronize your Shopify product catalog with Optimizely Connect Platform.

## Key Features

- **Complete Product Sync**: Import your entire Shopify product catalog to Optimizely Connect Platform as custom objects, including titles, descriptions, prices, and images.
- **Real-time Updates**: Webhook-driven updates keep your product data current as changes happen in Shopify.
- **Flexible Configuration**: Control which products to sync with options to include or exclude draft and archived products.
- **Simple Setup**: Easy authentication with your Shopify credentials and one-click historical import.

## How It Works

1. Enter your Shopify store URL and access token in the app settings.
2. The app validates your credentials and registers webhooks for real-time sync.
3. Click "Sync All Products" to perform your initial historical import.
4. Products stay in sync automatically through Shopify webhooks.
